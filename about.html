About CrossWorld League

CrossWorld League is one of the most modern and future-ready leagues out there. We run with our own custom bot and our own website, built to make things smooth, organized, and easy for players.

Our Mission

We aim to be a long-term, stable league that actually lasts and doesn't randomly shut down. Too many leagues come and go — we're here to build something that players can rely on for years to come.

Why Choose CWL?

Custom Bot

Our bot handles match scheduling, stat tracking, and automations to keep the league running smoothly.

Custom Website

A dedicated platform that organizes rules, events and team pages into a single place.

Future-Ready

We’re prepared for the upcoming Soccer Zero release and future growth.

Organized Structure

Clear staff roles ensure fairness and smooth event operation.

Long-Term Stability

We’re building something that lasts — not a short-lived project.

Modern Approach

Using the latest tools to create a better experience for players.
