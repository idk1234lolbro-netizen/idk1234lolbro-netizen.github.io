Staff Roles

Here are the roles of our server and a short explanation of each.

The Owner

Overall leader and final decision-maker for the league.

The Chairman

Supports the owner and coordinates high-level operations.

Head Manager

Runs events and manages daily league activities.

Lead Staff

Supports the managers and runs smaller projects and tasks.

League Admin

Handles organization, registrations, and system upkeep.

Moderator

Maintains a friendly, fair community and enforces rules.
