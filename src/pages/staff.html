Staff Roles

Here are the roles of our server and what they do.

The Owner

Overall leader and final decision-maker for the league (HammerOfThor).

The Chairman

Supports the owner and coordinates high-level operations (Meow).

Head Manager

Runs events and oversees daily league operations.

Lead Staff

Assists managers and manages projects.

League Admin

Handles registrations and organizational systems.

Moderator

Maintains a fair and friendly community.
