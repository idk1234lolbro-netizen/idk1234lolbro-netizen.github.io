About CrossWorld League

CrossWorld League is one of the most modern and future-ready leagues out there. We run with our own custom bot and our own website, built to make things smooth, organized, and easy for players.

Our Mission

We aim to be a long-term, stable league that actually lasts and doesn't randomly shut down. Too many leagues come and go — we're here to build something that players can rely on for years to come.
