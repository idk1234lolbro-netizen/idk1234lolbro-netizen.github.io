About CrossWorld League

CrossWorld League is one of the most modern and future-ready leagues out there. We run with our own custom bot and website to make things smooth, organized, and easy for players.

Our Mission

We aim to be a long-term, stable league that actually lasts and doesn't randomly shut down. Too many leagues come and go — we're here to build something players can rely on for years to come.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Custom Bot: Handles match scheduling and stats seamlessly.
  • Custom Website: Easy access to all league info.
  • Future-Ready: Prepared for Soccer Zero.
  • Organized Structure: Clear staff hierarchy.
  • Long-Term Stability: CWL is here to stay.
  • Modern Approach: Latest tools & technologies.
