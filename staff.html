Staff Roles & Descriptions

These roles help run CrossWorld League smoothly. Most leadership roles are community-driven and may change over time through votes and events.

Owner / President

The top authority of CWL. Responsible for overall league vision, major decisions, final approvals, and direction of the league.

How to get: Fixed role for the creator of CrossWorld League (hammerofthor).

Chairman

Assists the Owner in decision-making, oversees league operations, helps manage the website, bot, and internal systems.

How to get: Assigned by the Owner. Currently occupied.

Head Manager

Manages the staff team, coordinates events, schedules matches, and ensures all departments are active and organized.

How to get: Special role. Only a limited number can exist at once. Selected through community voting during events. The limit may change.

Lead Staff

Senior staff members who supervise specific areas such as matches, moderation, bot management, or website updates.

How to get: Staff application required. Selected based on experience, commitment, and community trust.

League Admin

Handles daily administrative tasks, moderates the server, updates records, and assists Lead Staff with operations.

How to get: Promotion from Moderator based on activity and reliability.

Moderator

Ensures community rules are followed, assists members, and reports issues to higher staff.

How to get: Open to active and trustworthy community members through applications and votes.

Most staff roles are influenced by community voting. Stay active, be respectful, and build trust — your reputation matters.
