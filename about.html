About CrossWorld League

CrossWorld League is a modern Roblox league built to deliver a fair, organized, and exciting Soccer Zero experience. Founded and owned by HammerOfThor and chaired by Meow, CWL uses its own bot and website to manage events, rewards, and member features.

Our mission

To create a community where competitive play and good sportsmanship come first. We want matches that feel meaningful and events that reward engagement.

What we offer

  • Custom bot features (economy, rewards, admin tools)
  • Organized events and mod-run activities
  • Clear rules and a team-focused system once the game launches
