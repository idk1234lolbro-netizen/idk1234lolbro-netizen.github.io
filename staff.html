Staff Roles & Descriptions

Most roles will be selected by member votes, so make sure to be friendly and active!

Owner / President

About: Top authority of CWL. Responsible for overall league decisions, vision, and final approvals.

How to get: Fixed position for the creator ‚Äî hammerofthor.

Chairman

About: Assists the Owner in decision-making, oversees league operations, and helps with website & bot tasks.

How to get: Assigned by the Owner ‚Äî meow.

Head Manager

About: Manages staff, schedules matches, and coordinates events. Special role: Only 5 members can hold it at a time (number can change).

How to get: Selected through special events and member votes.

Lead Staff

About: Senior staff supervising lower-level staff, manages departments, and reports updates.

How to get: Apply via staff application. Selection based on commitment, experience, and member votes.

League Admin

About: Handles daily admin tasks, moderates the server, updates records, and assists staff.

How to get: Can be promoted from Moderator based on activity, trustworthiness, and CWL knowledge.

Moderator

About: Ensures community follows rules, assists players, and reports issues.

How to get: Open to active and trustworthy members, selected through events and votes.
