Staff Roles

Here are the roles of our server and what they do.

The Owner

Overall leader and final decision-maker for the league.

The Chairman

Supports the owner and coordinates high-level operations.

Head Manager

Runs events and manages daily league activities.

Lead Staff

Supports managers and runs smaller projects.

League Admin

Handles registrations, organization, and upkeep.

Moderator

Maintains a safe, fair community environment.
