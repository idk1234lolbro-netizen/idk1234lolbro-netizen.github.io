Meet Our Staff

Roles will most likely be selected by member votes, so be friendly and active in the community!

Owner / President

About: Top authority of CWL. Responsible for overall decisions.
How to get: Fixed (hammerofthor)

Chairman

About: Assists the Owner, oversees operations, helps with bot & website.
How to get: Assigned by Owner (meow)

Head Manager

About: Manages staff team, schedules matches, coordinates events.
How to get: Only 5 at a time, selected via member vote events. Number can change.

Lead Staff

About: Supervises lower-level staff, manages departments, reports tasks.
How to get: Staff application required, voted by members, experience needed.

League Admin

About: Handles daily admin tasks, moderates, updates records.
How to get: Can be promoted from moderator based on activity & trust.

Moderator

About: Ensures rules are followed, assists players, reports issues.
How to get: Active community members, apply via events & vote.
